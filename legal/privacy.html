CHAIN Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

CHAIN is a daily word game. This policy explains what information the app uses and why.

Information we use

If you create an account, CHAIN stores your email, authentication record, public username, Daily score, word count, and board result identifier. Local game progress and preferences are stored on your device.

Services that process data

Supabase provides authentication and the live leaderboard. RevenueCat processes subscription status and purchase records. Apple and Google process store payments. Google Mobile Ads provides opt-in rewarded videos and uses its consent system before ads are requested.

Public information

Your chosen username, Daily score, and number of words can appear on the public leaderboard. Your email is never shown there.

Your choices

You can change your username or permanently delete your account from Profile inside CHAIN. Account deletion removes your CHAIN profile and leaderboard scores. Apple, Google, and RevenueCat may retain transaction records where required for fraud prevention, accounting, or law.
